Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

EdiShorts ("we", "our", or "us") is a UPSC exam preparation app that provides curated editorial summaries, analysis, and practice questions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the EdiShorts mobile application (package: app.edishorts.upsc).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name and email address when you sign in with Google.
  • Usage Data: Editorials read, bookmarks saved, quiz attempts, and in-app navigation patterns.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and unique device identifiers for analytics.
  • Camera and Photos: Only for the answer-evaluation feature. Images are processed and not stored permanently.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the EdiShorts experience.
  • To track your reading progress and quiz performance.
  • To send relevant push notifications (opt out anytime in device settings).
  • To improve app stability through anonymised crash and performance data.
  • To process subscription payments via Razorpay; we do not store card details.

3. Third-Party Services

EdiShorts integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policy:

  • Google Sign-In: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Razorpay: https://razorpay.com/privacy/
  • Expo / EAS: https://expo.dev/privacy

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us. Data is removed within 30 days of a deletion request.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encrypted transmission and access controls to protect your data.

6. Children's Privacy

EdiShorts is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of push notifications via device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of EdiShorts after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
